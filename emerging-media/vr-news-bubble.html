VR News Bubble
Social VR concept for connecting across our filter bubbles

Overview

What if you could peer into a stranger's social media feed? VR News Bubble is a social VR concept for connecting people across diverse social and political networks. VR News Bubble was presented at CMU's Open Studio Night in Fall 2016.

Project Description

  • Team: Michael James and Emily Saltz
  • Skills: Paper Prototyping, VR Prototyping, 3D Scanning, Scene Design
  • Tools: Unity, Blender, Photoshop, Structure scanner, ItSeez3D
