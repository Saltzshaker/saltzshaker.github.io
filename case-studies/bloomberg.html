Understanding Financial Data Nonvisually (In progress)

Overview

People with visual impairments are at a major professional disadvantage in finance because most data is presented in visual graphs. I'm working with a team of five to research and design a solution to close that gap.

Project Details

Role

Interaction Designer & UX Research Lead

Methods

Contextual Inquiry, Creative matrices, Experience prototyping, Book Design, Survey Design

Tools

InDesign, Illustrator, Sketch, Mural, Google Forms, Google Sheets, Keynote, Streak CRM

Note

This is a seven-month capstone project for Carnegie Mellon's HCI Master's degree with Bloomberg as our client sponsor, and is a work in progress. Our team of five completed the research phase in May and is currently on site in New York prototyping, user testing, and building solutions until August 2017.
