About


Hi, I'm Emily, UX Professional and media geek. I jump at every opportunity to diagram out the knots of a messy idea on a whiteboard or parse through layers of jargon. Went from studying Linguistics and Russian literature to wrangling design and content for Bay Area startups. Now getting my Master's at CMU designing for new media, including conversational user interfaces and virtual reality. I'm a sucker for good scrollytelling data journalism, and think a lot about the balance between innovation and accessibility.


You can find me on the Internet under the moniker of "Saltzshaker" or drop me a line at essaltz [at] gmail.com. Looking for Product Design and UX Research positions for fall 2017.
